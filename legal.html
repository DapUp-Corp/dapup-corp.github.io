DapUp offers an optional feature that enables new users to establish a direct network connection with the Company's co-founders, Jake Cameron and Brian Eddelson (collectively, the "Founders"), upon first use of the App. This section describes how that feature works and the data practices associated with it.

A. How the Feature Works

When you elect to use the "DapUp with Jake and Brian" feature within the App, the following actions occur automatically and server-side upon your affirmative tap of the designated button:

  • A friend connection request is submitted on your behalf to each Founder's account (user IDs 574 and 620) and is immediately accepted by the DapUp system.
  • A group messaging thread is created containing your account and both Founders' accounts.
  • Automated welcome messages authored by each Founder are inserted into that group thread on their behalf at the time of group creation.
  • You are navigated directly into the group messaging thread within the App.

This feature is entirely voluntary. You must affirmatively tap the "DapUp with Jake and Brian" button to initiate it. No connection is made without your explicit action.

B. Data Collected and Used

In connection with this feature, DapUp processes the following data:

  • Your user account identifier, authentication token, and session credentials, solely to authenticate and execute the connection.
  • Your account profile information, which becomes visible to the Founders' accounts as part of the established connection, consistent with standard connection visibility described in Section 3(B).
  • Group messaging data, including the group thread identifier, member list, and message content, which is stored on DapUp's servers consistent with the data retention practices described in Section 6.

  • DapUp Corp. is not liable for any expectation of personal communication, networking outcome, or business opportunity arising from use of this feature.

You may remove your connection with either or both Founders at any time using the standard connection management features of the App, consistent with your rights under Section 4(A). Removal of the connection does not retroactively delete prior group messages, which remain subject to the data retention policy in Section 6.
