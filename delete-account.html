Delete Your DapUp Account

DapUp Corp. respects your right to delete your account and associated data. You can request account deletion in two ways:

Option 1: Delete in the App

Open DapUp, go to your Profile, tap Settings, and select "Delete Account." Your account and associated personal data will be permanently removed.

Option 2: Request by Email

Email us at contact@dapup.app from the email address associated with your account, with the subject line "Delete My Account." We will process your request within 30 days.

What Data Is Deleted

Upon account deletion, we permanently remove your profile information, contacts data, connection history, messages, and location data. Some anonymized, aggregated data that cannot identify you may be retained for analytics. Data required to be kept for legal or regulatory purposes will be retained only as long as legally required.

For questions about your data, see our Privacy Policy or contact contact@dapup.app.
